Disruptive technology these are technologies which are relatively new but with the potential to completely change the way things are done. The McKinsey Global Institute 2013 report on Disruptive technologies defined them as Advances that will transform life, business, and the global economy. (JManyika, Chui, Bughin, Dobbs, Bison, & Marrs , 2013). The technologies include among others, mobile internet, internet of things, cloud computing and Automation of knowledge work which are crucial for the operations and functioning of block chain technology.
