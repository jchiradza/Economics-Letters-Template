On the other hand, if the VAT WHT agent is involved, in the worst case scenario, the client loses 55 days access to revenue if the transaction was done at the beginning of the month. In fact the access is transferred from the seller to the VAT WHT agent who has access until the 15th of the following month which translates to 45 days. Considering that those buying from non-Agents will not have this access to state funds removed, there is high probability of uneven operating environment for all taxpayers, with agents benefiting.
